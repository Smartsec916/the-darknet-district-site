About The Darknet District

The Darknet District is an underground cyberpunk venue built for those who resist the algorithm. Founded by the elusive Admin and maintained by his android companion Iris, the District offers an immersive escape from corporate control.

Inside, you'll find a retro-futuristic arcade, VR play zone, oxygen bar, retail store stocked with gear they don't want you to own, a banned books library, and a designated smoke/vape area for MetaMist and more. Every corner is wired for rebellion — part experience, part resistance.

No sponsors. No surveillance. Just the signal.
